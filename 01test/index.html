Digital Citizenship

What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship is basically how you use the internet and technology in a safe, responsible, and respectful way.

What is Digital Law?

Digital law is the set of rules and laws about how people use technology and the internet.

What is AUP?

An Acceptable Use Policy is a set of rules that explain how you are allowed to use technology, devices, or the internet—usually at school, work, or an organization.

What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is when you use someone else's work, ideas, or words and pretend they are your own.

Plagiarism means copying someone else's work without giving them credit.

What is Academic Dishonesty?

Academic dishonesty is when a student cheats or is not honest in their schoolwork.

Academic dishonesty means breaking the rules to get better grades or credit.

What are the rules for AI generated content?

In school, AI should be used as a learning tool, not to do assignments for you. Students should follow their teacher's rules, be honest if they use AI, and make sure their work is in their own words.
