About Me

Introduction

Hi, my name is Rebecca Conklin. I'm a 23-year-old, lifestyle, beauty, and motherhood content creator based in Utah. I'm currently pursuing a degree in Digital Marketing with a minor in Digital Media at Utah Valley University.

As a mom to my 21-month-old son, my journey through motherhood has deeply shaped my perspective and inspired the content I create. Through sharing my everyday life, I've discovered a true passion for content creation—connecting with others, expressing creativity, and building a community around authenticity and real-life experiences.

My Passion

Through content creation, I've discovered a true passion for storytelling, creativity, and connection. I love creating content that not only looks visually appealing but also resonates with others on a personal level.

My goal is to build a community centered around honesty, creativity, and real-life experiences while collaborating with brands that align with my values and lifestyle.
