Who were the Signers

Famous Signers

Here are some of the most famous signers of the United States Declaration of Independence:

John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large, bold signature.
Thomas Jefferson
Main author of the Declaration of Independence and later U.S. president.
Benjamin Franklin
Inventor, writer, and diplomat who helped gain support for independence.
John Adams
Strong supporter of independence and later became the second U.S. president.
Samuel Adams
Leader in organizing resistance against British rule.
Roger Sherman
One of the few individuals to sign multiple founding documents.
Robert Morris
Financier who helped fund the American Revolution.

There were 56 total signers.

These men helped create and support the United States becoming independent
